Greek Pasta Salad

This pasta salad will have you craving a trip to Mykonos!

Easy, light, and satisfying flavors come together to have you saying "Opa"!



You will need:

  • Dry pasta
  • Olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Sundried tomatoes
  • Grape tomatoes
  • Yellow bell pepper
  • Sweet red onion

Putting it together:

  1. Boil the pasta for ten minutes. Drain.
  2. Chop the tomatoes, pepper, and onion.
  3. Put the drained pasta in a mixing bowl.
  4. Add a block of sliced feta.
  5. Add the vegetables.
  6. Chill in refridgerator for 1 hour.
  7. Serve.
